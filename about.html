Meet the Crew

We are a team of dedicated professionals who love animals and are committed to providing the best care for your pets.

Co-owner/Stylist

Alex loves to helps animals look and feel great. He specializes in animal safe fur dye and can create fantastic and unique styles for your pet.

Co-owner/Hotel Attendant

Emily believes that every pet should feel at home even when they're away from home. It is important that pets feel comfortable and safe while staying with us, and Emily is dedicated to making that happen.

Manager

Haley is the manager of the establishment. Dedicated to helping pets and owners alike, she's always there when in need. Whether it's a question about services or a concern about your pet's stay, Haley is here to help.

Stylist

Kind and patient, Sarah is great for shy and timid friends during their grooming visits. She is dedicated to making their experience a happy and comforting one, ensuring that they leave looking and feeling their best.

Reptile Stylist

Have you always wanted your reptile to have the latest styles in hats and wigs? Well, Jeff is the stylist for you. Having studied across different countries over the years, Jeff has learned how to style reptiles in a way that is safe and comfortable for them. He is dedicated to making sure your scaly friends look their best.

Hotel Attendant

Zac is great at making pets feel welcomed during their stay. He always seems to know how to make even the most shy pets open up and feel comfortable. Zac is great with a variety of animals, so even if you have a ferret, bearded dragon, or snake, they will feel right at home.

Hotel Attendant

Gideon is the man who can get the job done. Having worked with pets over the years, and having experience with different breeds, personalities, and animals, you can be rest assured that your pet is in good hands with Gideon.
